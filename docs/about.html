About Private Captcha

A better CAPTCHA isnt just smarter - it's kinder

If you're running a website, you know the drill. A constant battle against spam, fake accounts and drained server resources. For years, the go-to solution has been CAPTCHAs. But let's be honest, many of them are a pain.

Your visitors are simply tired of the CAPTCHA grind. They've had enough of those moments where they're ready to sign up, buy, or engage, only to be stopped dead by a frustrating puzzle that feels more like a test of patience than a security measure.

That's why I founded Private Captcha. It's a solution proudly developed by Intmaker OU, a small, independent, bootstrapped company based in Estonia. Our mission is to build a safer web by making bot protection simple and private.

Values

What we stand for

A modern CAPTCHA should reflect modern values — privacy, fairness, and usability.

Privacy-first approach

Creating software that is not designed for surveillance is not only a pleasure, but also brings legal benefits of compliance with strongest data protection regulations like GDPR and CCPA

Pricing by quantity, not quality

All of our tiers have access to the same features and the pricing is based on usage. This does not force you to upgrade to a more expensive tier because it has the feature you need.

Captcha that everyone can use

Private Captcha is designed for self-hosting, allowing everyone to have modern protection. Our widget is accessible and without cognitive puzzles, to delight your users.
